Automated Spray Path Planning Based on Bayesian Optimization and Ant Colony Optimization

Ta-Jen Peng, Peng-Jen Chen, Ting-Lun Su, & Chung-Chieh Lin

The International Journal of Advanced Manufacturing Technology, Springer, Vol. 139, pp. 5491‚Äì5509, August 2025

This paper presents a novel automated spray path planning method combining Bayesian optimization with ant colony optimization for robotic forging applications. The proposed approach achieves significant improvements in production throughput (27%), defect reduction (91%), and cost savings (21%) while maintaining high trajectory accuracy (97.3%). The system enables flexible manufacturing line switching with substantially reduced setup time.

Research Statement

I am focused on AI-driven robotics and intelligent manufacturing, integrating Large Language Model (LLM) applications and automation research with practical industrial implementations.
